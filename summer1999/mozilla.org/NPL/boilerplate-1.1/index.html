License Boilerplate

This table contains preformatted versions of the boilerplate text that goes at the top of files which are licensed under the MPL or NPL. Please always use these templates for your code: they are carefully formatted so that future updates to the boilerplate will be easy to make.

Usage:

  • Use the "Save Link As..." feature in your browser to download them; don't copy/paste directly from the browser window.

  • All ______ fields must be filled in with appropriate text.

  • No sections of the boilerplate text may be removed or changed.

  • When changing a file, be sure to update its Contributors section as well as the years of copyright, if appropriate.
