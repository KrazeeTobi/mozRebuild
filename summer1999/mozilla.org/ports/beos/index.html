Mozilla on BeOS

Richard Hess <rhess@engr.sgi.com>

This project is an attempt to bring Mozilla (as defined by the Netscape 5.0 source code release) to the BeOS. One of the primary goals of this project is to leverage the strengths of the BeOS in order to provide an enhanced version of Mozilla for this platform...

Date: Thu, 25 Mar 1999 16:00:00 -0800 (PST)

In the past month Be, Inc. has stepped up their support of the Bezilla project, providing us with a full time engineer; Duncan Wilcox (of Blanket fame). Duncan has recently come up to speed and has greatly accelerated the progress of the port.

This support is in addition to the fine assistance that Be, Inc. has given over the past year to volunteer developers in the form of software and developer/engineering support.

We'd like to thank them for their continued support and willingness to see this project to completion...
