           Welcome to the Wonderful World of Wallets

The wallet feature "remembers" data that you have entered on forms and pre-fills that data on future visits to the same form or on other forms that ask for the same data.

You need not pre-specify your data, but you certainly can if you like.  If you would like to pre-specify your data, click here

To try filling out some forms, click on any one of the samples and then select either quick prefill or safe prefill from the wallet submenu:

      amazon.com                                   ebay.com

      bmgmusicservice.com         freeshop.com

      cdnow.com                      musicblvd.com

      classifieds200.com          netmarket.com

      coolsavings.com              outpost.com
