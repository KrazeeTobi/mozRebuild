Test Case Description for Image Loading (networked).

Purpose:

To confirm that images load directly into the web browser from a networked server.

Initial Conditions:

Disk and memory caches to be cleared.

Steps/Description:

1. View vlpan12.gif.
2. View vlpan11.jpg.
3. View wall_ni.png.

Expected Results:

After step 1, a 530x462 GIF image of the surface of Mars should display.
After step 2, a 1496x1536 JPEG image of the surface of Mars should display.
After step 3, a 640x480 PNG image of the Great Wall should display.
