rdf: references

Contact: Chris Waterson (waterson@netscape.com)
Last Updated: 18-March-1999

This is a dumping ground for RDF related references. Suggestions and new material gladly accepted.

    RDF Model and Syntax Specification. This is the "official" W3C Recommendation for the RDF syntax standard. Know it. Love it.

    RDF Schema Specification. Another proposed W3C Recommendation, this one for RDF's basic schema.

    The W3C RDF pages provide links to RDF projects, software, talks and documentation.

    RDF and Metadata, by Tim Bray. A high-level introduction to RDF.

    RDF Made Easy, by John Cowan. Another nice overview.

    The RDF-DEV mailing list, for archives of previous discussions.
