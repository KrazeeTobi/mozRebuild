reporting bugs

Mozilla developers and users of our binaries (nightly builds and milestone releases) are welcome to enter bugs about the source into Bugzilla, the bug database.

If you're an end-user of Netscape Communicator, Doczilla or Neoplanet, please report bugs to the developer who provided you with your executable. We'd like to keep Bugzilla a place where developers can talk about bugs with the core mozilla code base.

To submit a bug, just go to the Bugzilla bug entry screen. The first time, you'll have to go through a simple registration process.

You should also take a look at our bug writing guidelines, for guidelines and suggestions.
