Simple Bugzilla Search

This doesn't have all the bells and whistles of the real Bugzilla Query Page but some people thought it might be useful.

This search form does a case-insensitive substring search, and looks for open all bugs that have the words you enter in the fields you choose. For example, if you choose to look in Summaries only and type "crash", it will find bugs that have "crash", "crashes", or "crashed" in the Summary.
