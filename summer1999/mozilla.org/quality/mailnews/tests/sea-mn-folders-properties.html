Top-level folder with no subfolders
Notes
 Expected Outcome (if not obvious)
1 Check folder name . .
2 Check unread message count . .
3 Check total message count . .
4 Check wasted space Probably easiest to test with IMAP delete model, because you have visual indicators of the number of messages to be compacted, but eye the numbers with other delete models to make sure they look appropriate. Percentage to be compacted should approximately equal the percentage of the folder those messages take up, I think in disk space.
5 Check total space used . .
