bugs

Welcome to the mozilla.org bug center!

  • Are you a Netscape Communicator user with a bug to report? You're in the wrong place: please head on over to netscape.com instead.

  • Are you a Netscape Communicator user looking for technical support? Head on over to netscape.com.

  • Are you a developer who would like to browse the database of bugs reported to mozilla.org? If so, please visit the mozilla.org bugzilla.

  • Are you a developer who has a bug to report? If so, please visit the mozilla.org bug reporting page.

  • Have you tried one of our milestone or nightly builds and want to report a bug? Visit Quality Assurance and read their bug writing guidelines before you report bugs.

  • Do you want to look at the source for our bug system, and maybe use it for your own bugsystem? If so, check out the Bugzilla source page.

If you're a developer, and you're not sure whether some behavior you're seeing is a bug, you might want to discuss it with the relevant module owner, or in the appropriate newsgroup or mailing list.
