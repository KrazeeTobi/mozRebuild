The source is in the hands of the public. The CVS repository is up and running. The question is "what are you going to do about it?"

We're trying to collect some of the better answers to that question here in an ever growing repository of good ideas. Some of these ideas came into being as discussions on mozilla-wishlist, others were submitted by thoughtful readers like yourself and the rest have been lurking around the authors' collective subconcious ever since the first time we found ourselves mumbling "if I only had the source."
