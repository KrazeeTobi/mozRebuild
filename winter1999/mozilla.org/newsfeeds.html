news administrator information

Some sites maintain their active file (list of accepted newsgroups) by synching from neighbor sites or from well known lists such as the one available from the ISC FTP site. Other sites maintain the active file by accepting control messages. Here is the information needed to accept control messages for the netscape.* hierarchy.

If your news server verifies PGP signed control messages, you should add this to the key ring:

Next, add these lines to your control.ctl file: 

    ## NETSCAPE
# Contact: news@netscape.com
# *PGP*   See comment at top of file.
checkgroups:news@netscape.com:netscape.*:verify-netscape.public.admin=miscctl
newgroup:news@netscape.com:netscape.*:verify-netscape.public.admin
rmgroup:news@netscape.com:netscape.*:verify-netscape.public.admin

If you do not use PGP, use these entries in control.ctl: 

    ## NETSCAPE (Netscape Communications Corp)
# Contact: news@netscape.com
#
checkgroups:news@netscape.com:netscape.*:mail
newgroup:news@netscape.com:netscape.*:doit=newgroup
rmgroups:news@netscape.com:netscape.*:doit=rmgroup
