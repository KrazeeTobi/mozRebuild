Goals

The first goal is clear: make it work. There are several hurdles to be jumped in that task, and some actions items fall neatly out from there. See strategy.

What happens after then is not so clear, but there are plenty of things that can be done. What gets done will depend entirely on who is doing the work. We'll get to that topic later.
