XPToolkit Project

Vision: We make cross-platform user interfaces as easy to build and customize as web pages.

Elevator Story: straight up or touchy-feely.

Who are we, and what are we doing here?

How do I get plugged in?
We want all our discussions to be open to the net, and captured for the benefit of those who join later. We need your help.  Please discuss anything/everything about this project on the XPFE newsgroup.  If we're not keeping you in the loop, YELL at our manager. It's probably his fault anyway!
