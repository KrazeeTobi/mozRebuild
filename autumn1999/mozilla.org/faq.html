FAQ

Here are pointers to a few FAQs relevant to what we're up to here at mozilla.org.

Mozilla FAQ

Pat Gunn has put together this FAQ of common build problems, etc.

NGLayout/Gecko FAQ

Answers to questions about Mozilla's layout engine.

Netscape Public License FAQ

Answers to questions about the Netscape Public License, the license under which the Mozilla source code will be shipped.

Mozilla Crypto FAQ

Answers to frequently asked questions about the Mozilla source release and its support (or lack thereof) for cryptographic technology.

Free Navigator FAQ

Answers to general questions regarding Netscape's January 22 announcement that Netscape Communicator and Navigator are now free, as well as very general Q&A about the plans to release source code.

Open Source FAQ
Answers to some specific questions from the developer community regarding Netscape's plan to release source code for its next major release of Netscape Communicator and Navigator.

The mozilla.org Press Release
Netscape's announcement of the creation of mozilla.org.
