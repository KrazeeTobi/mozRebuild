XPApps? Isn't there a XPFE? What gives?

The XPFE has split into two groups: XPToolkit and XPApps. The XPToolkit group got their webpages up first, so they were able to claim that they are the new XPFE. However, XPApps and XPToolkit will be closely tied together. We may even end up writing a bit of each other's code.

XPApps will build an application layer on top of the XPToolkit. The application layer will include a shell and applications services. Our group will design and implement the XP applications that have to do with browsing, such as the browser shell, bookmarks, and preferences. Other groups will leverage off our shell and application services to build applications like the editor, and the mail & news client.
