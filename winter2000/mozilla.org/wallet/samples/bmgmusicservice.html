How you hear from us

Now when you join the Club, you can choose how you want to receive your club materials—through postal mail or your email. It's about selection. The Club that gives you the best choice in music now gives you even more choices!

Email - If you choose this option, Featured Selection and sale information will be sent to you every few weeks via email and you can respond to them at the site. You will be a member of BMG eClub, our new full-service electronic club.

Catalog - If you choose this option, you'll receive a catalog as well as information on your Featured Selection and sale offer via postal mail. You can still come to the website to respond.

Once you become a full member, you will be able to change your mailing preference by updating "How You Hear From Us" in your Member Profile.
