Description: Drag and Drop

For the most part, this test plan will not be able to be completed before we have some idea what the UI will look like. This document will be written as information about UI becomes available, but will include test cases for such things as Drag and Dropping:

  • News messages to various types of folders
  • Newsgroups to trash
  • Newsgroups to places they shouldn't be dropped
  • News messages to places they shouldn't be dropped
  • trying to undo drag and dropping
  • multiple folder and message dragndrops at once (contiguous and noncontiguous)
